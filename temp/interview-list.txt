Interview list
- Basic English, introduction and overall experience
- Functionality, pen if testing is required
- Priority vs severity
- Mandatory field used when writing test case, explain each
- Steps of test plan, test case, test execution
- Automation experience
- Tool used
- Basic terms of QA, definition. Ex functional testing, regression, production, retest
- Agile and Scrum, 4 ceremonies
- Different between waterfall and iterative
- Environment knowledge
- How to report bug
- Strong/ weakness of yourself

Main focus
- Capability to gain knowledge, open-minded
- Get along with team
- Understand standard of testing is the key.

Avoid
- Skip question, try to answer from your experience as much as possible
- Answer without good listening, keep catching the question and able to ask them to repeat the question
